Leadership

Leadership 7 Sept 2021

Leadership is a big responsibility. We go from roles that revolve around technology, to a role that is primarily concerned with social skills. However, there are similarities; we still care about finding the optimal solution to a problem. It's just that this problem is making the team the best it can be.

As well as the potential prestige, power, and pay rise, we're now responsible for a group of people. We can't control them as we can control software, nor should we try. People work best when trusted to do their job as they understand it. This can be a big change for developers moving up to leadership position, and it's easy to get wrong and begin micromanaging.

However, if we use our skills of learning and problem solving, we can become great leaders. This series contains some of my thoughts, observations and experiences on leadership.
